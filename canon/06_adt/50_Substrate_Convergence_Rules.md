50.1 The Three Convergence Requirements

All substrate processes must converge along:

1. Truth Convergence

Identity, vault state, and context all align to a single coherent truth.

2. Governance Convergence

Architect, MAIAi, and policies reduce to one authoritative decision.

3. Temporal Convergence

CSR ordering, ADT continuity, and execution finalization align into the next canonical moment.

If any one of these three does not converge, the system cannot proceed.
